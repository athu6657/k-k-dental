Our Dental Services

General Dentistry

We provide comprehensive dental care including check-ups, fillings, preventive cleanings, and oral health education to keep your smile healthy for life.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with treatments like teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, and reshaping. Designed to enhance the beauty and confidence of your smile.

Orthodontics

Correct crooked teeth, gaps, and bite issues with braces or clear aligners like Invisalign. Ideal for both children and adults seeking a straighter smile.

Dental Implants

A permanent solution for missing teeth. Dental implants restore function, aesthetics, and confidence with a natural look and feel.

Root Canal Treatment

Relieve pain and save infected or damaged teeth by removing the infected pulp and sealing the tooth to prevent future issues

Periodontics

Specialized care for your gums. Treat gum diseases like gingivitis and periodontitis to maintain strong gums and avoid tooth loss.

Oral Surgery

We offer a range of surgical procedures including complex tooth extractions, cyst removal, and other minor oral surgeries.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, friendly dental care tailored for children. We focus on prevention and education to build healthy dental habits from a young age.

Dentures and Partials

Restore your smile with full or partial dentures. Custom-made to fit comfortably and help with chewing, speaking, and facial aesthetics

3rd Molar Extraction

Safe and efficient removal of impacted or misaligned wisdom teeth to prevent pain, crowding, and infection

Smile Designing

Personalized smile makeovers using a combination of cosmetic techniques to create a perfect, balanced smile just for you.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation (FMR)

Tooth Jewellery

A trendy, non-invasive way to add sparkle to your smile. Quick application and easily removable when desired.

Flap Surgery

Advanced gum surgery to treat deep periodontal pockets and restore gum health, preventing further damage and tooth loss.

Dental Scaling and Polishing

Professional cleaning to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains, leaving your teeth smooth and sparkling.

Surgical Extraction

Performed when a tooth is impacted, broken, or difficult to remove with a simple extraction. Done with utmost care and precision.

Dental Crown and Bridge

Restore damaged or missing teeth with crowns (caps) and bridges. These fixed solutions are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Sinus Lifting

A procedure to add bone in the upper jaw near the molars and premolars, often required before placing dental implants
